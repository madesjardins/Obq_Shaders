Obq_Bend4Stereo


Class

Texture [Passthrough > Modify > Shader Globals]


Output

Color RGBA


Synopsis

Obq_Bend4Stereo is a passthrough node that changes the ray direction for camera rays. Used with stereo cameras, this allows the shading of the two cameras to be calculated from a single one.


Usage

Use the Obq_Bend4Stereo on all materials as a passthrough right before the "Surface" port of the material node or the "camera" port of a "ray_switch" node.


Fig.1 : In this stereo frame, the magenta and green in the highlights represent disparities between the render with and without bending.


Bend Mode


Bend Mode [bendMode]

How to bend :

•Don't bend:leave ray direction as-is.
•Bend from left:both cameras will be calculated as if coming from the left camera.
•Bend from right:both cameras will be calculated as if coming from the right camera.
•Bend from center:both cameras will be calculated as if coming from the center camera.

Cameras


Center [centerCamera]

The center camera.


Left [leftCamera]

The left camera.


Right [rightCamera]

The right camera.


CameraStatus [cameraStatus]

This shows the validity of the cameras.
